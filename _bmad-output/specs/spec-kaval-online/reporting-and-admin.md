# Reporting and Administration

## Dashboard (web, real time)

- Active cases by stage, offence type, area, staff
- Overdue visits count and list
- Interventions needed vs provided (gauge)
- Court sittings this week
- Travel claims pending approval
- Case intake trend — last 12 months

### Coordinator crisis queue (brainstorming — load-bearing for CAP-11)

Prioritized action queue replacing passive charts: overdue visits (critical), court within 48h without prep note (warning), recent handoffs (info), pending travel claims. One-tap to case and assigned worker.

## Standard reports

Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, yearly work reports; visits planned vs completed; overdue visits; interventions needed vs provided; court sittings scheduled vs attended; case counts by offence/classification/area; first-time vs repeat; staff workload and performance; travel allowance totals with ticket compliance. All exportable to Excel (.xlsx) or PDF.

## Master data (Legends) — CAP-12

Web admin panel; no Excel import required for legends. Immediate effect for all users.
